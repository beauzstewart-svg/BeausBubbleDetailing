About Beau's Bubble Detailing

Meet the person behind the brand.

Hi, I'm Beau Stewart

I'm the founder and CEO of Beau's Bubble Detailing — a sophomore at Highland Park High School, student-athlete, and entrepreneur.

I run varsity cross country and track, where I've developed a strong work ethic, discipline, and consistency that carry directly into my business. I've lived in the bubble my entire life and am actively involved in HPUMC, which has shaped my values and how I approach serving others.

This is my second successful business, and my focus has always been simple: delivering results people are genuinely satisfied with. While growth and profit matter, my priority is building something reliable, honest, and worth coming back to.

I believe in doing things the right way — putting in the work, staying consistent, and making sure every client walks away happy with the outcome.
